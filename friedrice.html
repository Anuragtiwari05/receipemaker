What all we need

Onion

Garlic

Green chilies

Soy sauce

Salt

Pepper

Oil

Spring onions (for garnish)

How to cook

To make fried rice, first, chop vegetables, onion, garlic, and green chilies.

Heat oil in a pan, saut√© the garlic, onion, and green chilies,

then add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.

Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper, and stir-fry everything together.

Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
