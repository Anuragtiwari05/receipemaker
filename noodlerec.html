SUSHI

Welcome chef Let's start cooking......!

What all we need

Sushi rice

Nori (seaweed sheets)

Rice vinegar

Sugar

Salt

Fresh fish (like tuna or salmon) or vegetables (like cucumber, avocado, or carrots)

Soy sauce (for serving)

Wasabi (optional)

Pickled ginger (optional)

How to cook

To make sushi, first, cook the sushi rice and season it with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.

Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice over it, leaving a small border.

Add fish or vegetables of your choice. Roll the sushi tightly using the mat, then slice into bite-sized pieces.

Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
