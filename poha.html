How to cook

Rinse and drain poha. Keep aside.

Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, let them splutter.

Add curry leaves, green chilies, and peanuts. Saut√©.

Add chopped onions and turmeric powder. Cook until onions soften.

Mix in poha, add salt, and stir well.

Garnish with lemon juice and coriander leaves.
