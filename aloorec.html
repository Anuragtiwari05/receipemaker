ALOO PARATHA

Welcome chef Let's start cooking......!

What all we need

Boiled potatoes (mashed)

Chopped green chilies

Finely chopped coriander leaves

Cumin powder

Garam masala

Amchur (dry mango powder)

Salt

How to cook

Prepare Dough: Mix wheat flour, salt, and water. Knead into a soft dough. Rest for 20 minutes..

Make Filling: Mix mashed potatoes, green chilies, coriander, cumin powder, garam masala, amchur, and salt..

Assemble Paratha: Divide dough into balls. Roll out one ball, place filling in the center, and seal. Flatten gently..

Cook: Heat a tawa. Cook paratha on both sides with oil/ghee until golden spots appear...
