KANDA BHAJIYA

Welcome chef Let's start cooking......!

What all we need

Chickpea flour (besan)

Rice flour (optional, for crunch)

Green chilies

Carom seeds (ajwain)

Cumin seeds

Turmeric powder

Red chili powder

Salt

Coriander leaves

Water

Oil (for frying)

How to cook

Slice onions and mix with green chilies, carom seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.

>Add chickpea flour and rice flour, mix well.

>Gradually add water to make a thick batter.

Heat oil in a pan and drop spoonfuls of the batter into hot oil.

Fry until golden and crispy.

Serve hot with chutney or sauce.
