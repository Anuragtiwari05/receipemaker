How to cook

Marinate Meat: Mix meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and spices. Rest for 30 mins.

Cook Rice: Half-cook basmati rice with salt and whole spices.

Cook Meat: Fry onions, add marinated meat, cook until tender.

Layer: In a pot, layer cooked meat, rice, fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.

Dum Cook: Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 mins.

Serve: Fluff gently and serve hot with raita.
